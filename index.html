A Cozy Cafe in Kadungalloor

Nestled in Kadungalloor, Kochi, our pastry shop offers a warm and inviting atmosphere. It's the perfect spot to unwind, enjoy a cup of coffee, and indulge in freshly baked treats.

We have **limited seating** to maintain a cozy and intimate experience for our guests. Please note that **we do not take bookings or reservations**; seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis. We look forward to welcoming you!
